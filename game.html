Clicker My clicker game for dummies

Indeed it is.

How to play

Click on the button, get more clicks, unlock autoclickers, convert those clicks to money, buy stuff with that money, including the autoclickers.

But I already have an autoclicker

I do not care. You want to butterfly click? Autoclick? Nozzle click? Or click normally? Your way of playing!

How are clicks counted

Every 100ms, the game updates and saves to localStorage, this is a tick. You could modify the amount of miliseconds-per-tick (t/ms) in the settings, but it isn't recommended at all.
