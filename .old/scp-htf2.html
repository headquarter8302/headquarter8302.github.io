SPECIAL CONTAINMENT PROCEDURES

SCP-HTF2 is currently contained on a prison cell inside D-Class sector [REDACTED] of Site-19. It is to be monitored 24 hours through either a nightguard, or a CCTV. A hay bale is to be placed inside SCP-HTF2's cell and should be replaced monthly.

In case of a containment breach, a team of Foundation serpentologist is to be dispatched to the scene immediately.

DESCRIPTION

SCP-HTF2 is a 3 meter long Opheodrys vernalis with it's head being the Heavy character from the Valve Corporation released game, Team Fortress 2. The bodily functions and components of SCP-HTF2 is exactly the same as the average Opheodrys vernalis species, the only difference and anomalous aspects of the anomaly is recorded from the "neck" area to the head. The head functions exactly like a human head, aside from the neck immediately terminating at the base of the skull.

SCP-HTF2 behaves as the same as a normal snake, the only anomalous aspect of it is the ability to eject bullets from it's fangs, as opposed to venom, which travels at the speed of approximately 850 meters per second. However, when SCP-HTF2 clings on to a prey, it would inject it's prey with smooth green snake venom, rather than the previously mentioned bullet.

SCP-HTF2 could resize, reshape and shrink itself to any size possible, becoming as small as 2 nanometers in length. The only way SCP-HTF2 could be contained is to make all sides of the containment chamber a prison style jail bars, and a hay bale where SCP-HTF2 could rest.
